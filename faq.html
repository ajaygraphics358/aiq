Course Structure & Content

Our classes are conducted through a hybrid learning approach:

  • Live Interactive Sessions: Weekly live sessions for real-time interaction
  • Pre-recorded Videos: High-quality video content you can access anytime
  • Hands-on Tasks: Daily practical assignments
  • Community Support: WhatsApp and Telegram groups for peer interaction
  • One-on-One Mentoring: Individual guidance when needed

This flexible format allows you to learn at your own pace while maintaining regular engagement.

The complete internship program spans 6-8 weeks:

  • Core Modules: 6 weeks (1 module per week)
  • Bonus Courses: Additional 2 weeks (flexible timeline)
